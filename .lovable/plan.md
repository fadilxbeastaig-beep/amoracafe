
# Amora Specialty Cafe — Premium Website

## Overview
A luxurious, modern single-page website for Amora Specialty Cafe with warm beige/cream/coffee-brown palette, smooth animations, and mobile-responsive design.

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Sticky Navbar
- Transparent logo (uploaded image) blended into design with soft opacity/shadow
- Links: Home, About, Menu, Reviews, Experience, Contact
- Dark mode toggle
- "Order Now" mock button
- Smooth scroll navigation

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width background with gradient overlay
- "Crafted Coffee. Perfect Moments." heading with elegant serif font
- Subtitle text, "View Menu" and "Visit Us" buttons
- Fade-in entrance animation

### 3. About Section
- Split layout: text + cozy cafe interior placeholder
- Highlights V60, cortado, Spanish latte, Ethiopian beans
- Scroll-reveal animation

### 4. Full Menu Section (Tabbed/Categorized)
- Categories: Specialty Coffee, Espresso Bar, Arabic & Traditional, Refreshers, Desserts, Bakery, Food, Healthy & Fresh
- Clean cards with hover animations (scale + shadow)
- "Best Seller" and "⭐ Recommended" badges on signature items
- Aesthetic food/coffee placeholder images per category

### 5. Signature Picks Highlight
- Featured cards for Spanish Latte, Cortado, V60, Devil's Cake, Nutella Cake
- Gold accent badges, premium card styling

### 6. Reviews Section
- Testimonial cards with star ratings
- 4.5 overall rating display with "35+ happy customers"
- Carousel or grid layout

### 7. Experience Section
- Three cards: Dine-in, Drive-through, Work/Study vibe
- Icons and lifestyle imagery placeholders

### 8. Instagram Gallery Preview
- Grid of placeholder aesthetic images
- "Follow us on Instagram" CTA

### 9. Location & Contact
- Address, phone, hours info
- Map placeholder (embedded iframe or styled box)
- Contact details with icons

### 10. Footer
- Amora logo + tagline "Made with love, served with passion"
- Social media icons
- Quick links

## Design System
- **Colors**: Beige (#F5F0E8), cream (#FAF7F2), coffee brown (#6B4F3A), soft gold (#C9A96E), dark (#2C2416)
- **Typography**: Inter/DM Sans for body, Playfair Display for headings
- **Animations**: Fade-in on scroll using Intersection Observer, hover scale on cards
- **Dark mode**: Toggle between warm light and rich dark coffee tones
- **Spacing**: Generous padding, premium breathing room throughout

## Technical Approach
- Single-page React app with smooth scroll sections
- Tailwind CSS for all styling with CSS custom properties for dark mode
- Intersection Observer for scroll-triggered animations
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Logo copied from uploaded image into src/assets
